If you are interested in becoming a Digitall Group partner, you can submit an application on this website.
All personal data you provide to us is completely secure. From this document you will learn what personal data we collect, how we use and protect it.

What does Digitall Group use your data for?

We need to pass your personal data to our sales managers for them to contact you. You can choose not to provide your personal data, but in this case we could not process your partnership request.
We add your personal data to our customer management system. Once this is done, we will contact you about our potential partnership.

What data is collected by Digitall Group?

We collect all the information you provide when booking on a travel supplier's website:

  • your name
  • email and phone number
  • your comment on the application
We do not validate your personal data that you provide to us.

There are data that are sent to us automatically without your participation:

  • your IP address
  • time zone
  • pages and links you have visited on our website
  • policy, name and version of your web browser and operating system
We collect and use the personal data of children under the age of 16 only with the consent of their parents or guardians. If we are found to be processing information about children without the consent of their parents or guardians, we have the right to delete that information.

How do we share the personal data we collect?

We only send your personal data to our sales department. If requested, we may share your data with law enforcement.

How does Digitall Group store and protect your data?

We use modern IT security tools and limit unauthorized access to our resources to protect your personal data. We are ISO-27001 certified, we use database encryption and we apply Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption when transferring personal data. We monitor and promptly resolve security issues that may arise due to external factors. If necessary, we will inform you of potential problems and tell you how to avoid them. Digitall Group and some of our partners are located in countries with the status of countries that do not ensure an appropriate level of data protection in accordance with the GDPR. However, we do our best to protect your personal data.

How can you manage your data?

We understand that you may want to manage your personal data provided to us. In any case, you wish to manage your data, do not hesitate to contact us via info@digitall.group

How Digitall Group Changes the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to the Privacy Policy and include the date of the last update. A new version of the document takes effect after its publication, unless otherwise specified.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are sent to your computer when you are on a website. They help us determine which pages of the website are effective and which need improvement.

We use cookies for marketing purposes:

  • to ensure that the website is working properly
  • to improve the website based on how you arrived at the website and the devices you use

The "How to Manage Cookies" section below describes how you can change your cookie preferences. Please note that if you completely disable cookies and similar technologies, it may affect your user experience on the Website, and some features may not work as expected.

What types of cookies we use

Below is a list of the different types of cookies that may be used on this website. This cookie policy describes how we use all kinds of information that may be collected from cookies.

We provide an expiry period for each of these types of cookies based on the information we have. However, the expiration dates for third-party cookies are set by these companies and may be changed by them at any time.

Basic cookie files
Basic cookies allow you to navigate our site and use our services and functions. Without these files, our website will not be able to function effectively.

Analytical cookies
Analytical cookies collect information about your use of the site and help us improve its functionality. For example, analytical cookies show us the most frequently visited pages on the site and analyze site traffic. We use analytics cookies to look at general trends in how people use the site, not how a user interacts with the website.

We use two analytics platforms: Google Analytics and Yandex.Metrics.

As cookie standards are continually evolving, we recommend that you check this policy from time to time to see if there have been any changes. The following parties place cookies and/or other technologies on our website:

  • Google
  • Yandex

Google Analytics services.

The Google Analytics service uses cookie technology. Google Analytics provides us with anonymous statistics. The service works with IP addresses, as well as information about the cookies that are used on our websites, so we learn how many people visit our site, how many of them browse certain pages, what browsers they use (which gives us improves the compatibility of our services for more people) and sometimes in which countries and regions users live. Sometimes IP addresses and cookie information are processed outside of the European Economic Area, and in that case we ensure that they are processed in accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act.

You can disable the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings in the browser.

Yandex. Metrics service.

We use the Yandex.Metrics web analysis service provided by Yandex LLC.

Yandex. The Metrics Service uses cookie technology. The information collected by the cookie does not identify you, but it can help us improve our site. Information about your use of this site, collected using a cookie, will be transferred to Yandex and stored on the Yandex server in the EU and the Russian Federation. Yandex will process this information to evaluate the use of your site, compile reports on our site activities and provide other services. Yandex processes this information in the order established in the Yandex.Metrics Service Terms of Service.

You can disable the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings in the browser.

However, this may affect the operation of some site functions. By using this site, you agree to the processing of data about you by Yandex in the manner and for the purposes indicated above.
